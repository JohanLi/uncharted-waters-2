About

This is a remake of Uncharted Waters: New Horizons, an open world RPG and simulation game released in 1994 and developed by Koei. The game is set during the Age of Exploration, dealing with sailing and trading. The remake is far from complete and merely a side project of mine, so any progress made will be sporadic. Visit my blog for updates. The code for this game is available on GitHub.

1552. The Age of Exploration. Three brave voyagers are each about to begin a life of adventure...

João Franco
As the son of Duke Leon Franco, João sails for the glory of Portugal. In his travels around the globe he hopes to discover the secret of the lost land of Atlantis.
Otto Baynes
A Royal Knight of the British Empire, Otto is on a secret mission for King Henry VIII. Sailing as a privateer, his goal is to defeat the Spanish Fleet.
Catalina Erantzo
This red-haired pirate hails from Spain. She seeks revenge on Portugal for the mysterious loss of two men — her brother and her beloved.
while others are determined to stand in their way.
Some provide help and support,
Now it’s up to you to guide them on the path to glory.
